Strategic Location

Maun serves as the gateway to the Okavango Delta, with proximity to Moremi, Sehitwa, Toteng, Shorobe, Chanoga, Makalamedi and Maun International Airport.

Implementation Strategy

Phased development approach ensuring sequential rollout and investment pacing

Phase 1 (2026)

CAPEX: ~BWP 650m
  • Bulk services and roads infrastructure
  • 5MW Solar PV installation
  • DEWATS wastewater treatment construction
  • 200 Bonno starter housing units
  • Transport hub setup
  • Initial commercial nodes

Phase 2 (2027-2028)

Full residential rollout
  • Mall construction
  • Island restaurants completion
  • Waterpark development
  • Business park activation
  • Tourism facilities implementation

Phase 3 (2029-2030)

Project completion
  • Apartments and final commercial layers
  • Full energy integration
  • Landscape completion
  • Long-term facility management
